Meet Jaymie

Passionate About Inclusive Beauty

I'm Jaymie Law, a Developmental Service Worker (DSW) specializing in autism and behavioural therapy with over 7 years of experience. I combined my love for helping others with my passion for beauty to create InJoy Beauty.

Through my work in the developmental services field, I've witnessed how something as simple as getting a haircut can be stressful or inaccessible. My mission is to remove those barriers and make self-care a calming, joyful experience for everyone.
